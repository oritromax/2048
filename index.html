How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Power-ups: Undo — Reverts the board to the state before your last move (keyboard: Ctrl/⌘+Z). Swap — Click the swap button, then click two tiles to swap their positions. Remove — Click the remove button, then click a tile to remove all tiles with that number from the board.
